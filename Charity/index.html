About Islamic Charity
Charity in Islam purifies wealth, erases sins, and brings blessings. Zakat is obligatory for eligible Muslims (2.5% of wealth), while Sadaqah is voluntary charity of any amount. Supporting Islamic projects is considered Sadaqah Jariyah (continuous charity) that benefits you even after death.
